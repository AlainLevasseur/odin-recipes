Simple Fried Rice

Description

A quick and easy fried rice. Makes enough for multiple meals of left-overs or to
feed multiple people. Eggs are optional and can also be substituted with
left-over turkey (which there can be lots of during the holidays!)

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of uncooked brown rice
  • 4 Cups of water
  • 2 Tbs of butter
  • 1 Large Carrot
  • 1 Can of peas
  • 1 Can of corn nibblets
  • Soya Sauce
  • 4 Eggs (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil rice, water, butter and a pinch of salt in a pot until rice is tender.
  2. Cube carrot into small chunks in a wok or high-sided pan.
  3. Saut√©e the carrots until begining to soften.
  4. (Optional) Beat the eggs and scramble on the wok, with the carrots on the side.
  5. Add the can of peas and corn to the wok.
  6. Add the cooked rice and sprinkle with soya sauce to taste.
  7. Mix the contents thoroughly until soya sauce is evenly coated.
  8. Bowl the rice and Enjoy!
