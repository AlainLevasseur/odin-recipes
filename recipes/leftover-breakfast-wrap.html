Leftover Breakfast Wrap

Description

This is the perfect meal to use up leftover breakfast ingredients from a
previous meal. Of course, it doesn't have to be left-over ingredients... No one
will blame you for using freshly cooked ingredients either! It's that good.

Ingredients

  • Soft tortilla shells
  • Omlette strips
  • Bacon slices
  • Sausages
  • Hashbrowns
  • Grated or sliced cheddar
  • Siracha mayo

Steps

  1. Reheat omelette, bacon, sausage and hashbrowns (in microwave).
  2. Place a soft tortilla shell on a plate.
  3. Spread the siracha mayo in a line on the tortilla.
  4. Add the cheese onto the mayo.
  5. Place the reheated ingredients onto the line of mayo & cheese.
  6. Wrap the tortilla tightly with the bottom folded over.
  7. Transfer the tortilla to a pan with the folded side down.
  8. Apply pressure while frying to ensure the tortilla fold is sealed and browned.
  9. Flip over and brown the other side.
  10. Let cool a bit and Enjoy!
